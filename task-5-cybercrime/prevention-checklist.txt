STAY SAFE ONLINE:

- Never share your UPI PIN with anyone
- Do not approve unknown payment requests
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links
- Always verify the sender before making transactions
- Use only trusted and official apps
- Enable app lock and security features
- Regularly check your bank transactions
- Do not scan random QR codes

UPI SAFETY TIPS:
- Remember that receiving money does not require entering a PIN
- Always double-check payment requests before approving

REPORT FRAUD:
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline Number: 1930
