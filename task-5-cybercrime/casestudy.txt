UPI fraud is one of the most common cybercrimes in India. In this type of fraud, attackers trick users into sharing sensitive payment information or approving fraudulent transactions.

Typically, the fraudster contacts the victim through a phone call, message, or social media. They may pretend to be a bank official, customer support agent, or online seller. The attacker then sends a payment request or a fake link, claiming that the user will receive money. However, when the victim enters their UPI PIN, the money is actually deducted from their account.

Students and young users are often targeted due to a lack of awareness about digital payment safety. These scams are increasing rapidly with the growth of online transactions and digital wallets.

The consequences of such fraud include financial loss, stress, and difficulty in recovering the stolen money if not reported immediately.
